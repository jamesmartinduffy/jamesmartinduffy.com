As someone who has always been into health and fitness, I was blown away by the number of medical studies that had been done demonstrating its potential benefits to a wide number of areas, including focus & concentration, memory, preventing illness, anti-fatigue, potential in treating several long-term diseases, and even improving male sexual performance (no, I'm not kidding).

We launched Auragin later that year, and fast-forward to today, it's become one of the top-selling ginseng products on Amazon.com. I have been taking it every day for the past two years, and swear by its benefits to focus, productivity, and preventing illness.
